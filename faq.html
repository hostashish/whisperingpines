All our tents have attached toilets and bathing areas. There is an option of Four Men Alpine Tents/Dome Tents with Sleeping Bag/Ground Bedding and are serviced by a common toilet and bath section.

Our resort is located in Kanatal, a picturesque village in the Tehri Garhwal district of Uttarakhand, nestled in the Himalayan mountains. It is situated approximately 300 km from Delhi, offering a perfect escape from city life.

You can reach us by road from Delhi. The road condition is mixed - some stretches are well-maintained while others can be rough. We recommend using a 4x4 vehicle or an SUV. The nearest railway station is Dehradun, and the closest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun.

We offer a variety of activities including nature walks, bird watching, trekking, camping, stargazing, photography workshops, meditation sessions, and local village tours. Seasonal activities may include bonfire nights, outdoor games, and cultural interactions.
