Whether you're chasing the angels or fleeing from demons, come to the mountains. You would agree with us that there can't be a greater bliss than to wake up to fresh peaks, dew drops on the leaves in the lush green fields! And there will be nothing better to make your nights spent serenading mother nature.

Morning mist, chirping birds, dew drops on the grass, majestic view of the Himalayan ranges and ethereal peace! That's what you will experience when you get up every morning at Whispering Pines Himalayan Retreat.

Come home to India's most loved mountain getaway. Surrounded by pine trees, apple orchards and Greater Himalayan ranges in the background, Whispering Pines is nothing short of a Hobbit World in itself with surprises waiting for you at every corner.
