The Whispering Pines Experience

Our Vision

Founded in 2015, Whispering Pines emerged from a vision to create an extraordinary escape that harmoniously blends luxury with nature's raw beauty. Our commitment to excellence has established us as the region's premier mountain retreat.

Strategic Location

Nestled at an elevation of 8,500 feet in Kanatal, our property offers panoramic Himalayan views while maintaining convenient accessibility from Delhi NCR. The pristine environment provides the perfect backdrop for an unforgettable mountain getaway.
